Stanford University · Cardiothoracic Surgery

Surgeon-scientist

After completing my General Surgery residency at AIIMS New Delhi, I joined Stanford's Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery as a Postdoc Research Fellow. My clinical research in lung cancer outcomes has been published in leading journals, and I have also delivered talks at major national and international cardiothoracic conferences. I serve as a peer reviewer for several leading journals including Nature Digital Medicine, Nature Precision Oncology, Nature Machine Intelligence, Computers in Biology and Medicine, and the Journal of Cardiothoracic Surgery. I now work at the intersection of medicine and machine learning—applying computer vision and NLP to advance clinical AI and healthcare.
